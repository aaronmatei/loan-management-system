# LendFest — Borrower User Manual

A step-by-step guide for **borrowers** using the LendFest customer portal to apply
for and manage loans.

> **One account, every lender.** With a single LendFest login you can borrow from
> many lenders (microfinances, SACCOs, chamas, individuals), track all your loans
> in one place, and build a credit score that follows you across all of them.

---

## 1. Create your account

1. From the LendFest home page, click **Create a free account** (or go to
   **`/portal/register`**).
2. **Step 1 — Your details:** first & last name, phone number, national ID,
   email, date of birth, gender. You can also add your **business & location**
   (business name/type, county, town, address) — optional but recommended.
3. **Step 2 — Set a password:** at least 12 characters with an uppercase letter,
   a number, and a symbol.
4. **Step 3 — Verify your identity:** upload a **profile photo** and **both sides
   of your national ID**. Lenders use these to verify you. *(If document upload
   isn't available yet, you can add them later from your Profile.)*

> **Already added by a lender?** If a lender created your account for you, just log
> in with your phone number and the password they gave you (usually your initials
> + ID number + `@<year>`, e.g. `Jd12345678@2026`) and change it. You'll be asked
> to upload your photo & ID the first time.

### Log in
Go to **`/portal/login`** and enter your **phone number + password**.
Forgot it? Use **Forgot password** on the login screen.

---

## 2. Find a lender

Open **Lenders** (`/lenders`) to browse every lender on LendFest.

- Each lender shows a **type badge** — **Microfinance**, **SACCO**, **Chama**, or
  **Individual** — and is **colour-coded by type** so you can tell at a glance.
- **Filter** by lender **type**, by the **amount** you want, by **maximum
  interest**, or search by name.
- Tap a lender to see its full terms (min/max amount, interest rate, duration).

### Link to a lender
On a lender's page, **Link** your account (confirm with your password). Once
linked, you can apply for loans with that lender. After you add your first lender,
the list focuses on real lenders you can borrow from.

---

## 3. Estimate before you borrow

Open the **Calculator**:
- Choose **which lender** (drop-down of your linked lenders),
- enter the **amount** and **repayment period**,
- see your **monthly payment, total interest, and full schedule** before applying.

Then tap **Apply for this loan** to carry the figures into an application.

---

## 4. Apply for a loan

From a lender (or the calculator), **Apply**: confirm amount, duration, and
**purpose** (e.g. business, school fees, emergency). Submit — the lender reviews it.

---

## 5. Track your applications

**My Applications** shows everything that hasn't been disbursed yet:

| Status | What it means |
|---|---|
| **Pending** | Submitted, waiting for the lender |
| **Under review** | The lender is reviewing it |
| **Approved** | Approved — **waiting for the lender to disburse** the money |
| **Counter-offered** | The lender offered a **different (smaller) amount** — your action needed |
| **Rejected** | Declined |

### Respond to a counter-offer
If a lender **counter-offers** a smaller amount, open the application and choose:
- **Accept** → the loan becomes **Approved** and waits for the lender to disburse.
- **Decline** → the application is closed.

> An **Approved** loan stays in *My Applications* until the lender **disburses** it.
> Once disbursed, it moves to **My Loans**.

---

## 6. My Loans

**My Loans** shows only loans that have been **disbursed** (money paid out):

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Active** | Disbursed and being repaid |
| **Completed** | Fully repaid |
| **Defaulted** | Overdue |

Filter by **Active / Completed / Defaulted** or by lender. Tap a loan to see its
**balance, repayment schedule, payments**, and to **download a statement**.

---

## 7. Make payments

- **Payments** shows your full repayment history across all lenders.
- Where enabled, pay by **M-Pesa** directly from a loan (you'll get an STK push
  prompt on your phone). Otherwise pay your lender as instructed; they record it
  and your balance updates.
- Every payment has a **receipt** you can view.

---

## 8. Your credit score & dashboard

Your **Dashboard** shows a **credit score** built from your repayment behaviour
across **all** your lenders, plus totals: borrowed, repaid, outstanding, and
on-time rate. Paying on time improves your score — and a good score helps you
borrow from more lenders.

---

## 9. Your profile

In **Profile** you can:
- update your email, date of birth, and gender,
- view/replace your **identity documents** (photo + ID),
- **change your password**,
- **download your account statement**.

Your photo, ID, and core details are shared across every lender you link to — so
you only enter them once.

---

## 10. Notifications

The **bell** icon shows updates: application decisions, counter-offers, payment
confirmations, and due-date reminders.

---

## 11. Quick troubleshooting

| Issue | What to do |
|---|---|
| "Invalid phone or password" | Use the phone number you registered with; reset via **Forgot password** |
| Stuck on "Verify your identity" | Upload all three images (photo + ID front + back) to continue |
| My approved loan isn't in *My Loans* | Approved loans live in *My Applications* until the lender **disburses** them |
| Can't see a lender after linking | Use the **Lenders** page; linked lenders are marked, and "See all lenders" reopens the directory |
| A counter-offer is waiting | Open *My Applications* and **Accept** or **Decline** it |

**Need help?** Contact your lender directly, or LendFest support from the site footer.
